## HOW TO UPDATE IMAGES
To change any image in the project:
1. Open src/config/imageConfig.ts
2. Find the relevant section (hero, gallery, tours, reviews)
3. Replace the URL with your new image URL
4. Save the file - changes will reflect throughout the entire project
